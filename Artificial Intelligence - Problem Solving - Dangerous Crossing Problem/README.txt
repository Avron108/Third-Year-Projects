Please note that the program initially has everyone on the RHS, and the goal is to get everyone on the LHS
Entry Order:
1. Number of persons - eg. 4
2. Name of person - eg. John
3. Time taken to cross for that person
(* 2 & 3 are repeatedly entered for each individual person)
4.The minimum time taken taken to get everyone across - eg. 17
5. Select the type of search desired, by entering the number represented for the search, as displayed. - eg. 1(for a BFS)
